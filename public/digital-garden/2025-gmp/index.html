2025 Global Market Portfolio

Created: March 2, 2025
Last Modified: March 2, 2025

For as long as I’ve been intentional about personal finances, I’ve been using this 2014 blog post to think about the global market portfolio. Time for a refresh.

Turns out there are way more resources available on this than the last time I checked in. (1, 2, 3.) The breakdown looks roughly like:

  • ~45% global equities
  • ~45% global bonds
    • ~21% government bonds
    • ~9% corporate bonds
    • ~5% securitized debt
    • ~10% emerging market debt/other
  • ~4% real estate
  • ~3% commodities (apparently 88% gold!)
  • ~5% private credit

Public markets-only version gets you to more like ~50% equities, ~42% bonds, ~4% real estate, ~4% commodities.
